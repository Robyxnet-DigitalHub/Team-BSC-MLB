frequently asked questions

What are entry requirements?

a.Admission shall be according to performance at BGCSE or equivalent as stipulated by the University with the specific requirement of a grade B or better in mathematics, chemistry, and biology or physics and a grade C or better in English, or must have obtained grade A for double science in lieu of the subjects listed here.
b.An applicant who holds Advanced Level passes in Mathematics, Chemistry and Biology/Physics with a grade C or better will be admitted directly into the programme.
c.An applicant who holds a Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology obtained from the Institute of Health Sciences or Its equivalent plus two years relevant experience and registered with Botswana Health Professions Council as a medical laboratory technician will be admitted directly into the programme and exempted from clinical attachment.
d.An applicant who holds a BSc degree in biological science/biochemistry or equivalent will also be admitted directly into the programme.

Job opportunities!!!

Medical laboratory science degree program provide students with the practical skills and knowledge they need to analyze data and conduct successful tests in a laboratory setting.
This type of training can prepare graduates for a wide variety of careers in applied medicine, medical research and biotechnology.
If you are interested in a career that allows you to apply your critical thinking skills and conduct essential medical research, then you might consider pursuing a medical laboratory science degree.
